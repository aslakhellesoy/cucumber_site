CukeUp!

CukeUp! 2011 was a smashing success, and it's happening again in London on April 4th 2012. It's a one day conference packed with half hour talks about all things related to the Cucumber BDD tool and ecosystem.

CukeUp! is organized by Skills Matter and Aslak Helles√∏y, the creator of Cucumber. Sign up at the Skills Matter web site.

Do you want to give a 30 min presentation? Send a proposal to with a title, a short abstract (about 50 words) and a few words about yourself.

Talks will cover topics like:

  • Cucumber with Ruby and Rails
  • Cucumber-JVM with Java, Groovy and other JVM languages
  • Cucumber-JS with Node.js and browsers
  • Using Cucumber with iOS and Android
  • Cucumber's cousin SpecFlow on .NET
  • How to keep Cucumber features maintainable and fast
  • Specification by Example
  • Browser testing
  • Related tools and techniques

This mixture of technical and non-technical talks should be beneficial for programmers, testers and people who write requirements.
